Autac Expands Into the Service Industry

Autac announces expanded service offerings including cord coiling from customer-supplied wire, custom cable manufacturing, and military specification work.

June 18, 2009
← Back to News

June 18, 2009 — NORTH BRANFORD, CT — We can coil cords from manufactured wire supplied by customers and also make and manufacture custom cables to meet your specifications. Send a cable sample to us, and we will coil the sample free. We also ROJ, Strip, and Tin each end of the cord as needed.

We also work with Military Specifications (CAGE#3AWWO).

We can design and build almost any type of cord that meets UL, CSA, RoHS, and other industry standards. With more than 61 years of experience, Autac is one of the largest distributors of coiled cords and expanded services in the wire & cable industry. We pride ourselves on customer service and quick turnaround times. Autac Inc distributes throughout the United States and Canada.
