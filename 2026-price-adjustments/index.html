2026 Price Adjustments

Important information about upcoming pricing updates for Autac USA retractile cord products.

Effective: January 1, 2026

Notice of Price Adjustments

Autac USA values the relationships we have built with our customers over nearly eight decades of business. We are writing to inform you that, effective January 1, 2026, we will be implementing modest price adjustments across our retractile cord product lines.

This decision was not made lightly. Over the past year, we have experienced significant increases in the cost of key raw materials, including copper conductors and petroleum-based polymers such as PVC, TPE, and polyurethane. These material costs directly affect every cord we manufacture. Rather than compromise on quality or shift production overseas, we have chosen to implement measured adjustments that allow us to continue delivering the same American-made products our customers depend on.

How This Affects Your Orders

The price adjustments apply to new orders placed on or after January 1, 2026. Here is what you should know:

  • Orders placed and confirmed before January 1, 2026 will be honored at current pricing
  • Existing quotes that have not yet expired will be honored at the quoted price
  • Blanket orders and long-term agreements already in place will be reviewed individually — your sales representative will reach out to discuss any changes
  • Updated pricing will be reflected in new quotes issued on or after the effective date

We encourage customers who are planning upcoming projects to request a quote or contact our sales team to discuss your needs ahead of the effective date.

Our Commitment to You

Even as costs rise, our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer service remains unchanged. Every retractile cord we sell is manufactured in our North Branford, Connecticut facility using American materials and skilled labor. We stand behind every product with the same confidence that has defined Autac since 1947.

We appreciate your understanding and continued partnership. Providing advance notice of these adjustments is important to us, and we are committed to working with you to ensure a smooth transition.

Questions?
