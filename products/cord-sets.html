What Are Cord Sets?
Power Cords, Ready to Connect

A cord set is a complete power cord assembly consisting of a flexible cable with factory-attached plugs or connectors on one or both ends. Autac manufactures cord sets in both straight and retractile configurations, with a wide range of plug types, wire gauges, and lengths to match your exact application.

Configurations
Cord Set Types

Autac builds cord sets for power, signal, and data applications. Choose a standard configuration or tell us your requirements.

↺

Retractile Cord Sets

Coiled power cord assemblies that extend and retract, keeping workspaces tangle-free.

  • ✓ Self-retracting coiled design
  • ✓ 1:5 extension ratio standard
  • ✓ TPE, TPR, PVC, or Neoprene
  • ✓ Molded NEMA plugs available
  • ✓ 2–25+ conductors
⚊

Straight Cord Sets

Standard non-coiled power cord assemblies with molded plugs for fixed installations.

  • ✓ Custom lengths available
  • ✓ Multiple plug configurations
  • ✓ Hospital-grade options
  • ✓ Right-angle and inline plugs
  • ✓ Color-coded jackets
🔒

Shielded Cord Sets

EMI-protected cord assemblies for sensitive electronic and medical equipment.

  • ✓ Spiral or braided shielding
  • ✓ Data and signal applications
  • ✓ Medical-grade available
  • ✓ Foil + drain wire options
  • ✓ Low-interference design
