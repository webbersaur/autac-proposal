▶ About this video

Learn all there is to know about coiled cable in this episode.

Autac is a manufacturer of high-quality coiled cords, or what is often referred to as curly cords. Since 1947, Autac has been producing custom-manufactured retractile cords to meet various applications and specifications. Autac was founded by Robert N. Burkle in 1947. Throughout his thirty years of running this company, his philosophy was simple — the customer comes first. Marie-Louise now continues her father's tradition as she moves Autac into the future.
