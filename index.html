Executive Summary

Autac Inc. has been a trusted name in retractile power cords and coil cords for nearly 80 years. What worked in 2021 ‚Äî a content-rich WordPress site with broad keyword coverage ‚Äî may no longer be the most effective approach. Opportunities

  • Content consolidation: The site has grown to 294 blog posts over several years. Google's algorithms have evolved ‚Äî they now favor fewer, more comprehensive articles over many posts targeting similar keywords. Consolidating overlapping content (e.g., multiple "coiled extension cord" and "retractile cord" variations) into definitive guides will strengthen rankings.
  • Tighter topical focus: Some blog content covers adjacent topics (badge reels, general cabling) that made sense as part of a broader SEO strategy but may no longer align with how Google evaluates topical authority. Narrowing the focus to Autac's core expertise will help Google clearly understand what the business does best.
  • Platform modernization: WordPress and Divi were the right tools when the site was built, but they carry overhead ‚Äî 200KB+ of framework CSS, JavaScript for page building, and database-driven rendering. Modern static sites achieve the same visual results with a fraction of the code, loading significantly faster.
  • Fresh core pages: The About, Contact, FAQ, and product pages haven't been refreshed since 2023. Updating these pages with current information and modern design patterns is an opportunity to make a strong first impression.
  • Streamlined user experience: Simplifying the site structure will make it easier for visitors to find what they need and take action.
